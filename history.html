The History



1878: Newton Heath Football Club was created

1902: The clubs name was changed to Manchester United

1945: Sir Matt Busby was appointed manager when the club were not successful

1958: The Munich Air Disaster occured which was devastating as 23 people of the club were left dead

1960-1970:Sir Matt Busby lead the club to secure multiple English titles.

1986: Sir Alex Ferguson is now the manager after success as Aberdeen manager

1992: The club wins their first league title under the new manager along with previous trophies

1998-1999: After winning a plethora of trophies Manchester United win their first treble whihc includes League title, Champions league and the FA cup.

2013: After winning 38 trophies for Manchester United Sir Alex Ferguson steps down as manager

2013: During Sir Alex Ferguson's time at Manchester United he spearheaded their success and transformation into a global powerhouse of a football club
