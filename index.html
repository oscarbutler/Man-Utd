About

Welcome to this website all about one of the most iconic clubs, Manchester United.

In this website you will be given knowledge on the rich history of the red devils and how they have become what they are now.

Through the late 1870's from where the club started to the present day.

There have been plenty of highs and lows for the club, players and fans.

Through all the happiness and pain the club have become one of the biggest and most appealing for fans all around the world.

There have been estimated to be over 100 million fans world wide.

In the last decade the support for the club has skyrocketed.
